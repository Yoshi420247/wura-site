Good to know

Before you book.

What should I bring?

Your unit (or buy one through Wura and we’ll match it to this booking), plus any inspiration photos. If you’re leaving hair out, come with it clean and detangled.

How does the deposit work?

You pay a 25% deposit to reserve your slot. It’s held in escrow and released to Adaeze only after your install is done and you’re happy.

What if I’m running late?

Message Adaeze through Wura. A short delay is usually fine; past 20 minutes she may need to shorten or move the appointment so the next client isn’t affected.

Can she source the hair for me?

Yes. Tell her the look you want and she’ll recommend a Wura-Verified unit, so the hair and the install arrive under one protected payment.

Do you offer home service?

For bridal and events, yes. Choose the home-service option and add your address at checkout. A travel fee may apply outside Lagos.
